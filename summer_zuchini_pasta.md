# Summer Zuchini Pasta

__for 1__
1 Zuchini
1 Lemon
1/2 onion
2 gloves of garlic
~40g Parmesan 
Pasta
~ 100ml Cream
Basilikum 

Grate Zuchini
dice onions
prepare 3cm^2 of lemon zest
prepare garlic for grating

sautee onions
add Zuchini
add zest
add grated garlic
reduce
add Cream
add parmesan 
let cool down for a minute 
add basil
