# Summer Zuchini Pasta

## for 1
- 1 Zuchini
- 1 Lemon
- 1/2 onion
- 2 gloves of garlic
- ~40g Parmesan 
- Pasta
- ~100ml Cream
- Basilikum 
- Grate Zuchini
- dice onions
- prepare 3cm^2 of lemon zest
- prepare garlic for grating

## instructions 
- sautee onions
- add Zuchini
- add zest
- add grated garlic
- reduce
- add Cream
- add parmesan 
- let cool down for a minute 
- rip apart basil and add
